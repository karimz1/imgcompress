## How this page works

!!! info "How this page works"
    This page highlights people who support ImgCompress and opt in to be listed.  
    - I add supporters through [Donations](sponsorship.md).  
    - I update this list when new contributions come in.  
    - If you prefer to stay private, I will not list your name or profile.

## How to get listed

!!! tip "Get listed"

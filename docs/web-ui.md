# Playing with the Web UI

Using **imgcompress** is easy. There are no complicated buttons to break. 

Make sure you have [set it up](installation.md) first.

## Quick 3-Step Guide

| Step | How it looks | What to do |
|-----:|------------|-------------|
| **1** | ![Choose your settings](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-upload-configure.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Drop your photos**<br/>Drag images into the box. Tell it if you want them smaller or in a new format. |
| **2** | ![Processing your images](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-processing.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Wait for the magic**<br/>Watch the progress bars fill up. All the work happens on your computer. |
| **3** | ![Download your results](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-download-results.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Get your new images**<br/>Save your fresh, tiny images one by one or get them all together in a ZIP file. |

## Fun with Backgrounds (Making Stickers)

Want to take the background out of a photo? It is like magic.

!!! note "Important Tip"
    To remove backgrounds, make sure you choose **AVIF** or **PNG** as your output format. These formats are great at keeping backgrounds invisible.

| Step | How it looks | What to do |
|-----:|------------|-------------|
| **1** | ![Toggle background removal](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-remove-background-toggle.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Flip the switch**<br/>Pick **AVIF** or **PNG**, then flip the "Remove Background" switch. |

## Meeting a specific size

Need your image to be exactly 1MB for a website? **imgcompress** can help. 

| Step | How it looks | What to do |
|-----:|------------|-------------|
| **1** | ![Choose format](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-select-format.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Pick a format**<br/>Choose **JPG** or **AVIF** so the tool knows how to calculate the size. |
| **2** | ![Add files](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-add-files.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Add your photos**<br/>Drag your images into the queue. |
| **3** | ![Set max size](images/web-ui-workflow/web-ui-set-max-size.webp){ .glightbox width="240" height="auto" } | **Set your limit**<br/>Open the "Set by File Size" section, pick your target (like 1 MB), and click convert. |
